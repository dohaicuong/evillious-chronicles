Prologue

Dear Kyle,

These days continue to be bright,
but are you spending them in good health?

Lately we’ve both been busy, and haven’t
had the chance to see each other much.

On that point I was considering holding
a dinner party for the first time in ages,

If you can, would you come to
attend?

Are you, like me, feeling run down
from having to do nothing but work?

I think it’s good to get free of
your responsibilities every now and then, and have fun with all your heart. I
want to host a magnificent party, so I think it would be good to use that as an
opportunity to broaden my circle of friends.

I sent out similar invitations to
the swordswoman Germaine and the sorceress Gumillia, so I think it best you all
come here together by boat.

I didn’t have time to arrange for a
big ship, so please make preparations for that yourself.

I’ll recoup your expenses when you
get here.

At any rate you likely won’t get
permission to board an ordinary ship, so this would be ideal don’t you think?

Truthfully there aren’t a lot of
days left until I hold the party.

So even though I imagine you are
quite busy, if you can,

Would you please return to Marlon as
soon as you are able?

I need no reply letter. You can
respond more directly by coming here.

PS. By the by, I hear you’ve been
taking quite good care of my daughter, Yukina.

I’d like to make sure I give you my
thanks for that too.

In…full.

Head of the Evillious Trade Association,
Keel Freezis
