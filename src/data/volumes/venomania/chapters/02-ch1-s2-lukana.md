Chapter 1 [Lukana Octo] Part 2:

Lukana Octo was a twenty year old female that owned a tailor shop that was in Mystica, the Southern part of Asmodean.

That was a village which was worlds apart from Capital Lasaland , positively speaking, it was a simple and carefree village, negatively speaking, it was a piece of uncultivated land.

Being born there, the charming Lukana that possessed an aura of elegance had always caught peoples’ attention ever since young.

Lukana was not born in a wealthy family, yet why did she had a charm that commoners did not? This was once the popular topic of gossipers. About the conclusion, people hypothesized that it might be due to her grandmother being a ‘Leviantan’.

Magic Kingdom Levianta — it was rumored that there was such a country south of Asmodean. Just as stated in the name, most of the citizens were ‘sorcerers’, having unimaginable powers and had appearances that could only be described as ‘beastly beautiful’. Levianta was destroyed a hundred years ago causing the number of sorcerers to dwindle to pathetic numbers. And majority would agree that most of the self-proclaimed ‘sorcerers‘ are actually just scammers.

Lukana’s ancestor might have just been one of the ‘sorcerers’. That would explain her uncommon aura of elegance.

And there was evidence to support this point. When Lukana was ten years old, she cried to her mother that she had a dream that ‘the village would face a drought’.

The rainfall of Mystica was already originally not high and the number of problems which was caused by the lack of water was also not little, thus, Lukana’s mother did not take her words into heart. She replied , “It is probably a nightmare that stemmed from the experience which you had when you were young about the problems caused by lack of water.”

Therefore, Lukana went around telling villagers about what she dreamt instead. But everyone acted the same like her mother, they refused to believe the words which was from a dream of a young child.

The only person who was willing to believe the child was a woman. Golden hair donned with a black robe, she was here to pay a visit to the nobles of Mystica, a guest of the Earl Ferdinand.

After the black robed woman heard about Lukana’s full dream, she left Mystica.

After that, Mystica was faced with a severe drought that they have never experienced before.

There was no rain for months, and the ground has cracked under the sun’s brilliance.

When the villagers finally believed Lukana’s ‘premonition’, everything was already too late.

Throats were dry, crops were unable to grow in the dry weather… when everyone was prepared for death, the black robed woman appeared once again.

She went to the ancient relic on the mountain that was near Mystica and took out an ‘eight-legged creature’ that no one knows where she attained from. She then started praying to the gods for rain.

It was as if the ceremony was taken as some sort of signal, rain had finally fallen down on Mystica for the first time in months.

The rain lasted three days three nights, ending the drought of Mystica. However, the mountain which the black robed woman had perform the ceremony on was forever enshrouded in thick fog, and the name thus changed to ‘Misted Mountain’.

Lukana who forecasted the drought and the ‘sorcerer’ who stopped the drought became the hot topic in Asmodean. However, ever since then, Lukana did not have any other ‘premonition dreams’, and the sorceress had also left the country, causing people to slowly stop discussing about it.
