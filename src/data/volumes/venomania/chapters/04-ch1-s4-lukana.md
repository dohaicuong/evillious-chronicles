Chapter 1 [Lukana Octo] Part 4:

Thinking about it carefully, a duke walking around on streets without servants is really strange. But the moment she stepped into the manor, Lukana understood the reason for it.

It was a spacious manor, belonging to another world that Lukana has never seen before.

The inside of it was full extravagant furniture, crafted with designs Lukana that she had never known.

Yet, there was not a single soul inside.

She suddenly felt the breath of someone near her and thus turned around to see, of course it was Sateriasis who was with her, yet the distance between the two of them was so small, causing Lukana who was slightly shocked to let out a small gasp.

“Ah! I’m sorry, I must’ve scared you.”

Lukana thought she saw a terrifying expression on Sateriasis’s face, but when she turned to look again, it has already returned to his usual calm expression.

‘I must’ve seen wrongly due to the dim lighting within the manor.’ Lukana decided and brushed it off simply.

“N-no… Are you staying alone in here? Not even a single servant was hired…”

“Un. That’s right. Ever since the massacre, I have been staying here alone.”

The reason why his clothing was so messy and why he was starving…

Simply speaking, it was all about this.

A son of a aristocrat cannot possibly maintain this manor all by himself.

“Even so, I still did all the things i could alone. I’m able to survive through this because i have money but it’s quite impossible to sustain the ‘aristocrat’s image’. This can’t continue on for much longer…”

Sateriasis sat down on the extra big main seat in the dining room.

After urging her, Lukana finally sat down at the smaller seat beside him.

“Why…do you want to live alone?”

“I lost my memories.”

“Yes…My uncle has mentioned it before to me.”

The information which Lukana’s uncle got from the female soldier seemed to be true.

“I should’ve seen the murderer’s true face because i was there but i just can’t seem to remember the face. I don’t know who that insane murderer is.”

“…”

“I’m a coward.”

Does this mean that he doesn’t want people he don’t trust by his side?

“Do you think that i’m strange?”

Lukana was unable to answer.

Sateriasis’s loneliness is something probably only he himself can understand.

“I didn’t ask clearly just now… but why were you strolling on the city’s street?”

“One reason was because the food storage became empty… the second was because i wanted to find people.”

“Find people?”

“Find females. I’m looking for two females, thinking that i might meet that if i were to stroll on the streets.”

“… Are they your lovers?”

Lukana covered her mouth with her hands the moment she finished the sentence, feeling that she gabbed too much.

… Why did this kind of question come out from her mouth?

Didn’t she just promise herself to not cross the boundaries?

Lukana chided herself.

Sateriasis smiled lightly when he saw Lukana’s panicked face before continuing.

“I only remember how they look like. One of them is a female with plaited white hair, the other has green hair and probably… likes wearing white western dresses. Do you remember seeing any of them? ”

“No… I’m sorry, but with just these information i have no clue either, and besides, I’m not from here.”

“That’s right…excuse me for asking such superfluous questions.”

“But since they have white and green hair as their characteristics, they should be from the Netsuma clan and a Elphe respectively. ”

Talking about the Evillious region, for people to be born naturally with white and green hair, they should be from these two races, both of them mostly belonging to the neighboring country, Elphegort. Even like this, there are numerous people from both races that actually travel between Asmodean and Elphegort for trade and other purposes so she thinks that it is quite difficult to find people with this characteristics.

“Speaking of Elphes… I remember that Duke-sama’s deceased mother was also an Elphe? ”

“That’s right. That’s why i think that they might be people who had known my mother.”

“I’ve heard that your mother’s senior…Marquis Glassred is currently handling the affairs of Asmodean.”

“You know a lot.” Sateriasis look at her with admiration. This was still Lukana’s homeland, of course she should at least know these. She was slightly unhappy but did not allow that to show on her face. After all, it was also the truth that she was from provincial areas that was not familiar with political situations.

“Because i’m still inexperienced, and furthermore have lost my memories, so i am really appreciative of the marquis’s help.”

“Why don’t you try finding Marquis Glassred… and ask him if he have any information about the Elphe female whom you are searching for.”

“Uhn…”

Sateriasis’s face darkened.

“After the massacre, we had an argument about me staying alone in the manor. This made it very difficult for me to visit him…and the Marquis has also stopped coming to the manor to visit me recently.”

“No matter what, I think that living this kind of life is a good thing. Just like what uncle said, Duke-sama’s health doesn’t just belong the Duke-sama.”

“… Yes.”

“Sorry… I was too nosy.”

“No, you weren’t. Thank you. What you say is correct… it suddenly feels colder in here.”

Sateriasis stood up and walked towards the fireplace behind of him, lighting it up.

The light from the fire made his body look blood red.

“Anyways, since i managed to purchase a set of good clothing, I don’t have to worry about it anymore.”

“No, that is only for temporary use…not something that Duke-sama should be wearing regularly. It’s better if you hired a tailor to properly tailor some clothing for you.”

“Is it? … Then, is it okay if i ask you to help me tailor some clothing, Lukana?

Sateriasis held her hands.

“Eh?! But, just me —”

“I really like the clothing you design. I hope that you can accept this job offer.”

Lukana looked down as she flushed red. The reason for her red face, was it because she was feeling hot from the fireplace; was it because of the embarrassment caused by the praise; or was it because Sateriasis was holding on to her hand?… Even Lukana herself doesn’t know.

But no matter what, this was just too hasty.

“Of course, i will be paying the amount you want.”

“… I stay at Mystica, if i were to remain here for too long, I will cause trouble for my uncle…”

“You can also leave your lodging’s payments to me. During the duration of making the clothing, if you want to stay in the guest room of the manor, it’s fine too. ”

“Eh?! No, but…”

“I know this is hard for you, but even so, i would like to ask you for it.”

At last, Lukana gave in to Sateriasis’s adamant request, promising him to sew new clothing for him.
