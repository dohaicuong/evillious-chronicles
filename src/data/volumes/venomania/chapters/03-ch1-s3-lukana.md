Chapter 1 [Lukana Octo] Part 3:

If she could freely control what she dreamt about, to make them into ‘premonition dreams’, would it be possible to prevent ‘that thing’ from happening?

Looking at the soundly sleeping male on the bed, she couldn’t help but to recall the incident from ten years back.

That was her first and only time she had a ‘premonition dream’. Even Lukana herself had not idea why she had the dream.

She supposed the sorceress that has saved the whole Mystica region would at least have an idea of what happened? About why Lukana had that dream and how she could make it happen again.

That sorceress seemed to be quite famous, because her name appears frequently in rumors. For example, how she saved an aristocrat of some country from some deadly illness or like how she saved Mystica, she saved villages on the verge of starvation etc…

However, ever since that time, Lukana has never met the sorceress ever again.

She looked out of the window. The sun was already setting but the festival was still bustling with people, instead it looked more and more crowded as night approached. A ‘festival’ is the same no matter where, since they often attract large number of people.

The boss of the tailor shop which she was in currently was her uncle’s. The few sets of clothing that she has tailored were placed within this Lasaland shop which was quite a distance away from the village of Mystica. After hearing the customers’ praises about the clothing Lukana made, and to match with the high demand of the New Year season, the shop ordered a large amount of clothing from Lukana. That was why this time, Lukana came down personally to the capital of Asmodean to pass the goods.

After her job is done, since it was a rare opportunity, she had decided to stay at Lasaland for a few more days to enjoy the New Year Festival before returning to Mystica.

The lively New Year festival is something exciting that cannot be experienced at Mystica. Lukana was enjoying the jubilant atmosphere thinking about how much fun they would have if she had brought her friends from Mystica along.

Just at this moment, she saw Duke Sateriasis Venomania fall in the center of the busy street.

Lukana had previously visited Lasaland many times and has also caught eye of the Duke a few times. During the times when the Duke was still Ilotte, his only son could still stroll peacefully and freely on these streets.

Tying his long purple hair into a pony tail, Sateriasis with his long eyelashes made him look as beautiful as a woman. He had a cheerful smile as he greeted the people cordially, making him look awe-inspiring, he was going to become a duke with a bright future.

However, the Sateriasis now was nothing like before. The clothes he was wearing was definitely not bad, one look and Lukana knew that it was made from high quality material that she would not have dreamed to even touch. But no matter how high quality the clothing was, without good care, it will still became crumpled and unpleasant.

Wearing the crumpled clothing really wasted his good looks —-

Just when Lukana was using a tailor’s perspective to feel pity for the high quality clothing, Sateriasis’s left hand moved slightly. His eyes opened slowly as he turned his face to look at Lukana.

“How are you feeling, Duke Venomania? ”

Lukana asked, Sateriasis’s unfocused eyes stared at her face, not long after, he started mumbling.

“Duke… Venomania? Is that… me?”

“Yes, it is you. You are duke Sateriasis Venomania, the lord of Asmodean. Are you fine? Is it still blur?”

“… Ah, yes. I am Sateriasis. Yes, that’s correct.”

Slowly sitting up, Sateriasis said as if he was trying to confirm a fact.

“Where… is this?”

“The ‘Octo’ tailor shop of Lasaland. Duke-sama fainted on the street just now.”

“Oh… Sorry about it then, I must’ve caused some trouble for you.”

“How are you feeling?”

When Lukana asked once again, Sateriasis’s stomach took the initiative to answer by growling.

“I’m fine… but it seems that i’m a little hungry”

“I will go and prepare some food immediately.”

The sight of him made Lukana exhale a breath of relief. Walking out of the room into the kitchen, she placed the ready-made food into a plate and brought it back into the room.

“I’m not sure if these food will be suited for your taste —”

“Itadakimasu”

Before Lukana even place the plate on the table, Sateriasis grabbed a bread from the plate and finished it in one gobble, the way both his cheeks puffed up made him look like a child.

“Un… It’s delicious.”

“This bread has ketchup and chili powder as its filling, actually i shouldn’t be giving Duke-sama such commoner food— ”

“No, it’s really delicious, un.”

Sateriasis praised the food she brought in finishing it in a blink of a second, and said somewhat guiltily,

“Sorry about it… But can i have another serving?”

“Ahh, seems like you’re starving.”

Lukana returned from the kitchen with another plate of ketchup bread, once again, Sateriasis finished it fervently. Finishing the tea that was also brought in together with the bread, he turned towards her.

“Huu… I’m full. Thank you, i’ve calmed down a lot.”

Sateriasis lowered his head and said his thanks, giving the impression of a rational person as compared to the one before.

“It might be a little rude … but can i ask you something?”

Lukana enquired gingerly. Even though it was none of her business, she was still curious about it.

“It’s fine, ask ahead.”

“Why were you walking through the streets in such clothing? And it seems as though it has been a long time since you’ve eaten.”

“Ah… Seeing the duke in this state must’ve been very strange…”

“I don’t stay in the capital, so there are things that i’m unsure of—”

“I see. Where do you usually stay at?”

“Mystica. I came to Lasaland for a work deal.”

“Oh, what is your job?”

“I manage a tailor shop.”

“That’s not bad, you must be an expert. I can feel it.”

“Aa, thank you… and then…”

Lukana cleared her throat lightly, pulling the conversation back to the topic at hand.

“I heard people saying things like ‘Duke-sama has gone mad’… Of course, I do not believe such words, especially now.”

Based on her deduction after having a conversation with the duke, she didn’t feel that Sateriasis was mentally ill. He looks extremely normal and even seemed very gentlemanly. After hearing Lukana’s words, Sateriasis looked down at his clothing, tilting his head to the back slightly.

“Oh, seeing this disheveled appearance, their thinking that i’ve gone crazy can’t be helped. Since that incident has happened.”

“…”

“Father, mother and everyone else… have all died.”

“…I heard that the murderer has yet to be apprehended.”

“Yes… Causing insecurity among the citizens, i feel guilty about it.”

“There’s no such thing! Duke-sama is the one who is in the most pain…”

Lukana answered as she scrunched her eyebrow together, tears glistering within her eyes.

She doesn’t know why she suddenly felt like crying. But, if she was placed in the shoes of Sateriasis—.

Your own house being destroyed, everyone around you getting killed—.

She thought of her father’s face, her mother’s face, the faces of her good friends Lilian and Rajih, and she thought about how she will feel if she lost them.

This way, Lukana can deeply experience how miserable it must be for Sateriasis who was going through this.

“…But, the fact that Duke-sama survived… is already very fortunate.”

She finally forced these words out of her mouth.

“You’re kind. Your name is…”

“…Haha. I forgot, i have yet to introduce myself.”

Lukana wiped the tears from her eyes and stood up straightly.

“My name is Lukana Octo, Duke-sama.”

“Lukana, was it? A very beautiful name. I am Sateriasis Venomania… Ah, you should already know.”

“Ahh.”

“And, I’m not very used to you calling me ‘Duke-sama’. Since it was a title that has been forced upon me.”

“Is that so?”

“After father got killed in the massacre, didn’t i suddenly take over the title of duke? If it is only the Earl who governs the country, many problems will arise, that’s why they allowed me to succeed his seat, it’s only for appearance sake. ”

Even Lukana who was a commoner knew, Asmodean belonged to the Beelzenian Empire, and the nobles there had a deep rooted mindset for tradition and appearances. She sympathizes Sateriasis’s plight that he had no choice whether he wanted to be a duke, but she also knew that it was something that she couldn’t change.

“If possible, I hope that you can change the way you address me. Otherwise, you can just call me ‘Sateriasis’.”

“That might be too… no matter what, to me, ‘Duke-sama’ will always be ‘Duke-sama’…”

“You don’t have to mind it that much.”

Sateriasis coaxed once again but Lukana was still unwilling to stop addressing him as ‘Duke-sama’. Even though he was much kinder and friendlier than what she have thought, he was still a noble, and there is a line that shouldn’t be crossed.

The door opened while the two were conversing, revealing the boss of the tailor shop, who was the uncle of Lukana.

“Ohoh. Duke-sama, you’re awake. Is your body feeling fine?”

“I’m fine, no problems at all. Sorry for causing you trouble.”

“Is that so, that’s good. Please pardon my rudeness, but to be cautious, it would be best if you get checked by a doctor. Your health is very precious, regardless to us or to you.”

“I will… sorry to disturb, thank you. Even though i’m empty handed now, I will definitely bring you a thank you gift someday. ”

After saying that, Sateriasis stood up slowly.

“You are returning? Not going to rest for a little longer?… Ah, to Duke-sama, staying in this small room must make feel breathless and constricted… ”

“That wasn’t my meaning, if i have caused any discomfort for the both of you, please pardon me. I just thought that it would be very rude if i continued to trouble the both of you.”

“The sky is already getting dark, please allow us to bring you back to your mansion… Lukana, can i count on you to send the duke?”

Lukana nodded mutely in acceptance to the question of her uncle. Due to the festival, there were still many customers in the shop, thus, the only free person in question should be Lukana.

“If you don’t mind, please change into this set of clothing.”

Uncle passed a set of male clothing to Sateriasis.

“This is the most high quality formal dress that we have, it should be more befitting than the ones you are wearing now.”

“…It’s really embarrassing, thank you for the trouble.”

Sateriasis took the clothing, spreading it open right at the spot.

“Un, the taste in the design is really good. The trimmings and embroidery really suit my taste.”

“From Duke-sama, members of Venomania family tend to like Marlon styled clothing.”

“Oh, then this clothing imported from the island nation of Marlon?”

“No, this is made when Lukana tried to imitate Marlon styled clothing. Even though the material is slightly worse off than the one that Duke-sama is wearing…”

“I see. My intuition is indeed correct… Lukana, you’re a talented tailor.”

Sateriasis said as he gazed at her, making Lukana’s cheeks flush red in embarrassment.

“I will send the cost of this clothing together with the thank you gift that i will be sending… I would like to change, is it okay for the both of you to leave the room for a while? ”

“Pardon us, please take your time.”

After finishing the sentence, both Lukana and her uncle left the room.

“Uncle.”

“Yes?”

While waiting, Lukana asked her uncle about information of Sateriasis.

“I once heard on the streets that ‘The duke has gone crazy’, but looking at him, he really doesn’t look like he is—”

“Ah, those are just baseless rumors. No matter where it is, there will always idiots who start these rumors, it’s just that— ”

“Just… what?”

“I heard that Duke-sama lost some of his memories.”

“Lost his memories?”

“This information was told to me by a female soldier who patronizes my shop.”

“Ah, you are talking about the person that uses words weirdly…”

“… That was a tragic event. The lord’s house was attacked, other than Duke-sama, most of his family were killed, there were even servants who were disfigured in their deaths. Experiencing that horrible event must’ve been quite a great blow, losing his memories is nothing surprising. “

“… But, in the end that is still nothing but a rumor, right?”

“Having said that, but the reliability of the words said by military soldier Tette Cetera who was in charge of the after matters of the incident are much more reliable than those rumors in the city— ”

At this moment, Sateriasis who have finished changing exited the room.

— The him now gave off a completely different impression. This made Lukana realize that the Duke who has changed into befitting clothing, was indeed an aristocrat, completely different from commoners.

‘Such a beautiful person’, Lukana whispered in her heart. The duke was indeed a bishounen, but it doesn’t just stop there. It even seemed like she was hallucinating, as if Sateriasis himself was emitting some sort of top class luster, so beautiful that it makes Lukana jealous as a woman.

‘If he were to wear even better clothes, this person would definitely become even more eye-catching.’ She thought to herself.

Perhaps it was because of these thoughts, when she was accompanying Sateriasis back to the manor before witnessing the state of it, she had completely forgotten to ask him why he was wondering around the streets of Lasaland.
