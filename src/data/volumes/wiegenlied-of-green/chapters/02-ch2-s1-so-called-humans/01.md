Section 1——The So-called Humans——

This forest is very peaceful.

This forest is very boring.

Eldoh-san said to me, it's a very good place here.

A thousand years have passed. I learned a lot of things.

About the flowers.

About the seeds.

About the stones.

About the springs.

About the wind.

About the soil.

But I have a lot of things I don't know.

Such as things outside the forest; I don't know them.

Humans in or outside the forest; I don't understand them.

So I have no idea.

Why that child is crying.

For me, I can't understand the point of it.

These "humans" are incredible creatures.

I would really like to know about them.
