There wasn’t a soul inside Riliane’s room.

The voice that I had been hearing from this room earlier was gone now.

…No, I could hear it. It was faint, but I could clearly hear it.

Where was it? Where was it coming from?

Here. It was coming from inside the fireplace. Was there a secret passage in a place like this?

The hole was a bit cramped for an adult to pass through. But, well, I would get through somehow or another.

I had to hurry, hurry and touch that hand-mirror.
