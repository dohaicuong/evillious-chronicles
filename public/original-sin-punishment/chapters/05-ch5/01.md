–I was the one crying, but it also wasn’t me.

.

After regaining her composure, Irina looked up to the ceiling and calmly said, “It’s over, Seth.” And then next shifted her gaze to the illusions of Ly and Milky. “…Or do I need to take on those two as well?”

After a short silence, she heard Seth’s reply.

“No, that won’t be necessary.”

The illusions vanished in an instant.

“You’ve already bested them. Those two lost to you earlier, after all.”

Seth’s voice had a much cooler tone than it did just a minute ago.

Perhaps he was displeased, or bewildered.

“So then, I can be queen now?” Irina pressed further.

After another brief pause, Seth answered, “Yes…But I do have something I’d like to ask.”

“Such as?”

“Just who…who exactly…are you?”

Thinking on what she should reply…Irina gave a derisive snort.

“I’m Irina. Irina Clockworker. A music box craftswoman, Kiril’s little sister. And…you must know my real identity.”

“…”

“I’m a ‘ghoul child’. One of your offshoots. My appearance and personality might be different, but the fact remains that I am one who has a ‘heart of malice’ just as you do.”

The silence lasted several more seconds.

And then, among the countless doors deeper into the temple…one of them slowly opened.

“…Come inside. Let’s talk face to face.”

There was no point in hesitating now.

Irina started to walk towards the door.

She didn’t even glance back at the corpse of the person who’d been her sister-in-law.
