“…She’s gotten away♥” Conchita said mournfully, while skillfully evading the claws swiping at her. Though, conversely, she was smiling like she was enjoying herself. “Ildebrando, honestly. Even after you’ve died you’re still causing me trouble.”

Conchita quickly seized the corpse’s right wrist with her left hand, then grabbed his left with her right. She then pinned him to the wall with all her might.

The corpse struggled to break free from Conchita. He shot his face forward and fiercely bit down on her neck. After letting out a groan she immediately let go with her right hand and used it to grip the corpse’s head, pulling it away from her.

Fresh blood spilled from her neck. But that was only for a moment. The fang marks that had been left there healed up in a flash, and the flowing blood ceased.

“You need to be punished, Ildebrando.”

Banica opened her mouth wide.

“Well then…what sort of taste do you have, I wonder?”

And then, in return for what had just been done to her, she bit down on the corpse’s neck.
