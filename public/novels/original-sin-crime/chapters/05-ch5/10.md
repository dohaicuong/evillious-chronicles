One night four days later.

Meta gazed at the babies that had been put in cylinders inside the “Baby Room”.

“Haha…They’re so cute.”

She’d never had any interest in children—or so she’d always believed. But when she actually stood before these babies, babies she had gone to such labor to birth, she felt they were quite precious.

It was too bad that Pale wasn’t the twins’ father.

And—that she couldn’t hold her children with her own hands.

Tomorrow the twins would be moved to the castle.
And eventually…they would be made into receptacles for the gods.

“Ultimately…you’re little more than experimental subjects.”

That was how she had come to feel, looking at them affixed with tubes.

“Just like my darling—”

.

–Then, what of herself?

That thought came into her mind, unbidden.

She couldn’t remember much of her past.

Where had she been born, and to whom?

A glass cylinder…A body affixed with tubes…

This should have been her first time seeing such a thing, but it felt familiar to her.

Pale…Darling…the man I love…

When she first met him, she had somehow felt this familiarity with him.

.

The girl in the cylinder—“Gretel”, had her eyes open.

She looked at Meta, and reached out her little hand.

Without even meaning to—Meta placed her palm over hers, through the glass.

In that moment—
Meta’s destroyed memories began to be reborn.
