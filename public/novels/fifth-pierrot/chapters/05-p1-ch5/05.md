That night, he had a strange dream.

It wasn’t his usual nightmare.

Two figures were talking inside a deep red space.

One looked like his mother, and the other looked like Rin.

“—Why doesn’t he use the ‘dead soldiers’?” his mother asked Rin.

“He doesn’t know how. Or rather, he doesn’t even realize that he can control ‘dead soldiers’ in the first place.”

“Then you should tell him. You’re better at using them than anyone.”

“It wouldn’t do him any good. He’s been that way forever. He’s just stupid. That’s why this time all he does is get carried away by other people’s opinions.”

“But then, he’s already made the contract. Let’s watch over how things play out now…I wonder what he’ll decide tomorrow. What do you think?”

“Don’t really care. Both of the women he’s considering joining are false mothers to him. He’ll just end up here in the end. And at that time, he’ll remember everything.”

“Like you, huh? –Oh? Over there…”

His mother looked his way.

So did Rin, a moment later.
