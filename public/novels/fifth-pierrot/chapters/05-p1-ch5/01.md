May 23rd. 1PM.

The commander of the Freezis Foundation, Aai Freezis, had arrived at the port of the capital Lucifenian in the Republic of Lucifenia. She was accompanied by her vice commanders Nob Nicole and Bruno Marlon.

The three of them boarded a carriage that had been prepared for them. This was to head for the Lucifenian palace.

They were planning to hold a conference there tonight with Lucifenia’s president, Julia Abelard.
