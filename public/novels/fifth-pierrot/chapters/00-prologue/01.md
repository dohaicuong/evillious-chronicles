The twins that fell from heaven wanted to destroy this world.

Everything began from there.

.

The older sister was named “Levia”.

The younger brother was named “Behemo”.

The twins were buried deep in the earth along with the culture of the previous era.

The kingdom that was established on top of it thrived through digging up that old culture’s legacy.

However, the humans unearthed something that was unwelcome too.

The ark the twins were sealed inside—“Sin”.

.

The twins were locked away in the ark, but there existed several humans who could perceive their consciousness.

Eventually one human who did such came to be called queen, and the twins were worshipped as gods.

–That was not wrong, but it wasn’t correct either.

.

The twins who had become false gods granted her a prophecy.

“We are considering destroying this world. If you wish to prevent that, you must reincarnate us.”

“Birth the Twins of God, who will become the receptacle for us.”–

.
The Senate, who worked under the queen, began “Project Ma” in order to create the “Twins of God”.

.

The “Twins of God” were safely birthed due to the second “Project Ma”.

A boy and a girl.

They were named “Hänsel” and “Gretel”, respectively.

.

To celebrate this the queen made the new “Evillious Calendar”, which had the year that the twins were born as its starting year.
