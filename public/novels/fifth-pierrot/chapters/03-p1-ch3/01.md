610 E.C., January 4th. 2AM.

The Republic of Lucifenia, 28th Division in the town of Rolled.

.

A man and a woman danced alone in the town that had become engulfed by darkness.

The man smiled, the woman’s face warped with terror.

Finally, she slowly collapsed on the spot.

A silver knife plunged into her well-rounded breast.

.

The black of dusk was stained with the red of blood.

The man smiled even wider. He laughed with a face covered in white makeup.

He had been playing the role of Pierrot.

In a circus of night without an audience.

.

No—there was an audience. But that audience had been killed by Pierrot, just now.

The dead woman had been a prostitute.

Counting her, there were eight murdered prostitutes in the town of Rolled.

But—he wasn’t finished yet.

.

This woman hadn’t been “Seventh, Magician” either.

Until he found her—

.

The circus of night would not end.
