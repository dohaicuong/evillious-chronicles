Extract from Year 609, October 1st Saintes Fauraux Newspaper 36th Edition

Milanais Theater slated to hold circus performance once more.

The performance held by the popular circus troupe, “Cirque du Lune” is sponsored by the merchant Sir Ton Corpa.

The members of Cirque du Lune are presently set up in tents on the premises of Corpa’s estate, and are said to be working hard on practice for the upcoming performance.

The first show is planned to begin October 10th at 6PM. Ticket purchase inquiries are to be sent in to the Corpa Firm.
