Gumillia and Michaela greeted Elluka when she had returned once more to the Millennium Tree.

.

Elluka looked incredibly exhausted.

“Welcome back, Elluka. …How was it?” Gumillia asked her.

“Hm? How was what?”

“What do you mean, what?…What about Margarita? What happened, to her?”

“What are you talking about, Gumillia? Margarita died back then, in Shadow’s mansion.”

“Huh? But—”

In that instant, Elluka’s eyes flashed green.

Neither Gumillia nor even Michaela had noticed that.

Next Elluka placed her hand on Gumillia’s shoulder, and murmured, “Margarita is dead. …Isn’t that right?”

“…Yeah…That’s true. I guess, I misunderstood, something.”

“You’re a bit of a scatterbrain, aren’t you Gumillia? –Now let’s leave already before it gets too much longer. We’ve gone pretty far out of our way but let’s go to Lucifenia for real this time. We have to look into ‘Pere Noel’.”

“You’re right. Let’s go, Elluka.”

Then Elluka turned to the Millenium Tree.

“And Michaela…When things calm down, we’ll come here again.”

“…Take care of yourself. …Hey, Elluka?”

“What?”

“You…are Elluka…aren’t you?”

“What are you talking about? Of course I am.”

“Yeah…I guess so. Well then Elluka, bon voyage.”

“Right.”

The Millennium Tree Michaela continued to watch as the two sorceresses became farther and farther away, until she couldn’t see them anymore.

.
