A black box. Several golden springs were jammed into it.

They were warped, and fissured, but even so they somehow continued to turn.

And they turned the disc, and played music.

.

But…it was the wrong melody.

Its step was slightly out of sync. It had an uncertain tempo. It was not the sound that had been envisioned by its creator.

The correct melody was lost to the shadows of history.

.

It was missing a gear.

The gear to play the “song of love”

I know where it is.

But I can no longer go there.

.

I must find them.

The one who will become a “Castaway of Time”.
