In the beginning

There was the gods’ paradise

.

The paradise that prospered for so long

One day abruptly greeted its end

.

The gods embarked on a large ship, and left their paradise

Found a new land

And made it their new paradise

.

The second paradise

Thrived the same way as the old one

But the peace only lasted

For about a thousand years

.

That was because in the new paradise

There existed something that had not been in the old one

And before they knew it, it undermined the gods

And eventually the paradise turned to hell

.

The gods named this “malice”

.

The ones who managed to escape from their crumbling second paradise

Were only four gods

And their sixty eight kin

.

They advanced along a black sea on their ship

And searched for a new place

And there

They once again tried to create paradise

.

–From the first book of the Levin Bible, “Genesis”
