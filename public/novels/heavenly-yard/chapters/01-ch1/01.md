The solitary Collector, Gallerian Marlon.

Created just before his death a small, small theater.

.

There was only one work that he screened there.

Just a documentary introducing his collection, the “Vessels of Deadly Sin”.

.

The one who served as the film’s director and screenwriter was the woman who had been his lover.

But it’s said that Gallerian himself died before the movie could be finished.

.

That theater deep in the forest eventually became a place that no one would approach.

And because of that, there were few who had actually laid eyes upon it.

.

That small, small theater.

The one acting as curator was the “doll” that Gallerian had loved most in his lifetime.

.

And that theater was called “Evils Theater”.
