Germaine raced up the stairs to the palace, calling out to Arth on the roof.

“Your Majesty, we’ve got a situation! The northern sky is—”

“…I know. I was just looking at it now,” Arth replied, gazing at the black clouds in the distant sky and the building that was floating in the center of them.

Then he turned to Germaine, and asked:

“How are the soldiers?”

“Most of them have recovered, but still not completely—"

“Well, fine. Just tell those who are able to move to start making preparations.”

“…You mean—”
“—To march.”
