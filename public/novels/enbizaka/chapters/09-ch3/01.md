In life there is freedom, and there is purpose.

Purpose is something that we find, that takes shape as we grow up.

That’s how it is for almost all people.

But I had already had a purpose given to me from birth.

Or, to speak paradoxically, I had been born for that purpose.

When I first told Inukichi about it, he had simply laughed at me.

Maybe he thought that I was joking.

That was reasonable for him.

I am special.

I believed that, and yet—

I had completely forgotten what that purpose was.

.

I met Kayo-san in Enbizaka.

At first I had thought that she must be my purpose.

But that had been a false purpose, given to me by a demon.

Ultimately I had not arrived at my true purpose, but by then I had to give up on it.

Sometimes missing someone by such a slight margin can completely change your fate.

Like with Gakuga.

I wasn’t able to meet Riliane.
It’s disappointing, but that’s just how things went this time.

I’m sure she’ll be reborn again. In some other country, as someone else.

I shall wait for that.

And that time for sure, I’ll see her again.

We have infinite time.

I can do it over again as many times as I need to.

.

But Kayo-san is different.

Unlike me, her time isn’t forever.

She had been a false purpose.

Even so, she—

She was a mother to me.
