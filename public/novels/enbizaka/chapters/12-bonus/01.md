Inside a mansion that was blazing brightly, he was finishing reading his last book.

It was a story of a place far, far east from here.

A story of the time period where his great-great-grandmother, Perrié Cutie Marlon, lived.

.

He could hear footsteps.

Someone was heading towards his room.

They were perhaps a messenger from Hell.

.

There weren’t any books left on his bookshelf that he could read.

Those remaining had all been burned up by the fire.

He’d tossed the book that he held in his hands into those flames too.

.

Then he suddenly realized.

That there was still just one book that wasn’t yet burning.

He didn’t have a lot of time left now.

Nevertheless, he picked up the book.

.

This too was a book that she had written.

A screenplay written by “Ma”.

It was different from the other books in that it was still halfway through being written.

This was the last screenplay that she’d penned, unfinished.

On the cover was written the following title:

.

–“Judgment of Corruption”–

.

He involuntarily made a wry smile.

He’d just noticed that the book was written about himself.

It was understandable for it to be unfinished.

Because his own story wasn’t over yet.

.

He sat down in a chair.

Nearby was his beloved daughter.

He opened to the page at the beginning of the book, and began to read it.

.

This karma of “evil” will not end.

And–

His own tale

Is now going to begin.
