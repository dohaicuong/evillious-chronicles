Perhaps to distract from the grief of losing her father, from then on Banica’s pursuit of food came to be even more refined yet.

She visited many, many countries and ate up every type of dish there. Around then, she ended up stretching her travels through not just the Evillious region where Beelzenia was located, but in the eastern portion of the continent as well.

Of course, it was to expand Beelzenia’s food culture even further, but more than that it was an action borne from her own desires.

To eat as many of the delicious foods around the world as she could—that had been Banica’s dream from long before.

Without a family or anyone she was in love with, there was only one thing that Banica could concentrate her passions on.

That was “food”.
