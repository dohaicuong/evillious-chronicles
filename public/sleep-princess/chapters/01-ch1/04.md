From the moment I was born I haven’t been able to sleep. That’s just natural for me, but it seems that’s not the case for other people. Especially when my father realized that I was a sleepless baby, he had been extremely surprised, saying things like “this baby could be a divine child” and “this is extremely interesting, looking from a medical point of view”. In spite of that event happening when I was a baby, somehow I can clearly remember him saying that of me even now. Maybe that’s because I really am a divine child. However, no matter how much my father investigated into it, he never found the source of my sleeplessness. Outside of the fact that I couldn’t sleep, I was a largely average child, and so apparently my father gradually lost interest in my condition. When I turned ten he came up with the vague conclusion that it was “a simple mutation” and stopped studying me. I had another purpose, so it seemed he decided to turn his attention in that direction. One day, he took me to a noble family who lived in the biggest house in town. That was where I first met him. He was a blue haired boy.
