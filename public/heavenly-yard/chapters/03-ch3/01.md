The heroes were dreaming.

This was an experience they had not done for a long time.

Their lives as they were recorded in history—there were some truths in it, and some falsehoods as well.

What was depicted in textbooks and movies were always just the portions that were convenient to the creator.

But this wasn’t the case in what the subjects themselves were dreaming about.

A reality that was at times quite cruel was confronting the heroes.

.

Red manacles.

That was surely someone’s blood.

Blue chains.

They were surely someone’s tears.

.

Their heroic tales were built upon such things.

And these had become shackles of the heart that bound them.

.

Inside of their slothful slumber.

One hero murmured in their sleep:

“Just what in the world is evil?”
