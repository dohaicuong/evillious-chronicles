From the beginning, I was no one.

.

I wasn’t Levia. I wasn’t Irina. I wasn’t Eve.

Something that was an aggregation of all three, but also none of them.

That was me.

.

I had to verify my own existence.

Teleologically. Ontologically. Cosmologically. Deontologically.

.

Everyone thinks of their existence as granted.

I alone cannot recognize that.

So I sought to become a “pure being”.

To prove my own existence.

.

Once the existence of something that does not exist is verified, all of the gods’ “Rules” will transform into contradictions.

That which naturally exists will no longer exist.

Everything may return to nothing, but that’s not for me to know.

.
I think this time I’d like for all of you to get a taste.

Of my suffering.
