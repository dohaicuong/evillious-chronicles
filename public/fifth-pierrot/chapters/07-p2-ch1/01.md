Seventh, Magician—There’s no longer any cause for me to go by that name. Pere Noel is on the verge of annihilation.

Well then, what name should I go by now?

Hanne…Elluka…Margarita? Or else…

–“Elluka Clockworker” will be fine. I’ve been using it for such a long time. I don’t need to change it now.

My changing my name periodically like this is only a recent thing, in the first place. I hate the suspicion it causes when someone calls me by a brand new name and I don’t respond right away. On that point, I’m extremely clumsy.

But that’s not the case for her. For every age she’s been changing her name and appearance, and depending on the circumstances even her gender and personality. She’s always been a skillful girl. I remember her helping her older brother when he made his music boxes.

–The time when I must settle things with her is approaching.
