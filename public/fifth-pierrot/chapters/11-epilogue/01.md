Year 611 EC, February 25th.

.

That day, Ayn Anchor was strolling through the town of Rolled while pushing along an old woman in a wheelchair.

The old woman was politely gazing at the appearance of the town, while making vacant eyes. She did also look as though she was smiling somewhat.

“…This street has become very tidy, hm? In the past there was more rubbish scattered around, so the road was pretty uneven,” the old woman murmured.

“Is that so?” Ayn responded.

The old woman told him she wanted to see Milanais Square.

It wasn’t so far from the place they were in now.

Ayn nodded, facing the direction of the square and pushing the wheelchair.

.

The old woman’s name was “Julia Abelard”.

She was the woman who had been this country’s president until just recently.
