Until evening he ate at the inn, bought two sets of clothes at the clothing store, and rested a little.

And then, for the first time in a long while he saw his usual nightmare.

His dream of being left in the water—Just who was that woman?

Just like always, she left somewhere after abandoning Lemy.

Without meaning to, Lemy cried:

“Don’t go—please, don’t abandon me—Mother!”

.

When Lemy awoke, he was in a cold sweat.

Rin was next to him, fast asleep.

Sitting up from bed, Lemy murmured, “Is that—a memory from when I was abandoned?”

Lemy thought of Julia, who was probably in Lucifenia.

What would she think if she found out that Lemy had killed someone?
Would she help him?

Or—

Shivering, Lemy once more burrowed under the covers.

.

I—

I don’t want to be abandoned again.
