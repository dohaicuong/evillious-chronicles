–When I think on it, mine was a life of only running away.

.

To cast aside the role you are given, and flee—

I think that, too, is a type of sinful “evil”.

.

These children are me, and they’re Pale.

That’s why.

I want to at least let these children be happy.

.

I run through a forest path where flowers scatter.

Two babies that I love held to my breast.

.

Yes, I—am a runaway.
