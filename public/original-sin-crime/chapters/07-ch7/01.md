When Adam left the house, he saw the charred corpse of a woman lying outside.

Tumbled down beside it he saw two small glass bottles full of liquid.

“…”

He dropped to his knees and checked the woman’s face.

Half of it was hideously burned, but not to the degree that he couldn’t tell who it had once been.

And once he realized that he recognized this face, Adam’s eyes opened wide.

“—Meta Salmhofer…”

The Witch of Merrigod.

The woman who had killed Adam’s colleagues and attacked the village that Eve had been living in.

Until this moment Adam had no idea that she had been living near their home.

“I see…So those twins…were your children,” he murmured, after which he scooped up the two bottles.

–There was milk inside.

And there was writing on the underside of each bottle.

“Hänsel…and Gretel.”

Those must have been the names of her children.
“…”

Adam stood, his heart heavy.

He would need to bury the body.

That would be the smallest of recompose for Meta, and would also cover up the crime that his wife had committed.

He took out his shovel from storage.

Then he went around to the back of the house and started to dig a hole–
